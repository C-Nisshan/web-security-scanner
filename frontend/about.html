About Aegis

We make web security accessible

Aegis was founded on the belief that every organisation — regardless of size — deserves enterprise-grade security visibility.

Our Mission

Security intelligence, delivered with clarity

Who we are

Aegis is a web application security platform built to give organisations a clear, continuous view of their attack surface. We combine automated discovery with targeted vulnerability analysis to surface risks before they can be exploited.

Our platform is designed for security teams, developers, and compliance officers who need accurate findings without noise — so effort is directed where it creates the most value.

From startups managing their first production environment to enterprises protecting complex multi-tier architectures, Aegis adapts to the scale and pace of your business.
