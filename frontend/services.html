What We Offer

Comprehensive security services

From initial discovery to detailed reporting, Aegis covers every stage of your web application security lifecycle.

Surface Discovery

Aegis crawls your entire web application, mapping every accessible page, form, and endpoint to establish a complete picture of your attack surface.

  • Breadth-first page mapping
  • Form and input enumeration
  • Broken link detection

SQL Injection Testing

Automated injection of SQL-based attack patterns to identify database exposure vulnerabilities that could lead to unauthorised data access or manipulation.

  • Error-based detection
  • Boolean and time-based probes
  • Parameter-level findings

XSS Assessment

Probes application inputs for reflected and stored cross-site scripting weaknesses that could allow client-side attacks against your users.

  • Reflected XSS detection
  • DOM-context analysis
  • Encoding bypass coverage

Risk Reporting

Every scan produces a structured report with severity-ranked findings, affected locations, and clear remediation guidance tailored for both technical and management audiences.

  • Severity scoring
  • Executive summaries
  • Developer fix guidance

Continuous Monitoring

Schedule scans to run automatically and receive alerts when new vulnerabilities appear — keeping you ahead of regressions introduced during development.

  • Scheduled assessments
  • Change detection
  • Trend analysis

Compliance Support

Align your security posture with industry frameworks. Aegis findings map to OWASP Top 10, helping you satisfy audit and regulatory requirements efficiently.

  • OWASP Top 10 mapping
  • Audit-ready outputs
  • Historical evidence trail
